## 🔍 Patient Segmentation & Key Drivers

- Applied K-Means clustering (k=3) to segment patients into risk groups  
- Identified high-risk clusters with elevated glucose and HbA1c levels  
- Used SHAP analysis to determine key drivers of diabetes risk  
- Found that glucose levels and HbA1c are the most influential features  
- Provided lifestyle-based recommendations for risk reduction
